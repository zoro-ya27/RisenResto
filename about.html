About Us

Risen's Restaurant is a family-owned restaurant that offers the best dishes in the city. Our goal is to provide the best dining experience to our customers. We have a wide variety of dishes that you can choose from. We also have a cozy ambiance that will make you feel at home. Come and visit us today!

Risen's Restaurant is a celebration of Filipino culture and tradition. Founded by Risen Crist a woman graduated with culinary degree, a passionate chef from the Philippines, it tells a story of humble beginnings, family recipes, and a love for authentic Filipino cuisine.

At Risen's, we invite you to experience the warmth, hospitality, and vibrant tastes that make Filipino food truly special. Welcome to our table!
